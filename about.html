Don Alcantara

We started out DJing house parties all over the bay area almost 20 years ago. From the house parties, we started doing various nightclubs in San Francisco and San Jose and from the nightclubs, a lot of the nightclub goers we DJ'd for started to get married and so we got into the wedding DJ business. It's been about 10 years since our first wedding and we've got to say that the 75 or so weddings since have been an enjoyable lesson each time.
