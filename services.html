Ceremonies and Receptions

We've provided sound and lighting at wedding ceremonies and receptions for many years entertaining as many as 1000 guests to small weddings that have only 10 guests. We know it's our client's most important day of their lives so we want to do our best to make it memorable as possible.

Whatever your needs are, we can accomodate. From a full detailed song list you create to letting us decide all the music, we will personalize your big day to fit your needs and all you dreamed your big day would sound like.

Poppin Bottles!

Our DJs have been spinning at nightclubs for over 15 years up and down the west coast to Las Vegas. We've played for thousands of club goers playing all the club anthems every loves to spend their weekend dancing to and hanging out and catching up with friends. From Hiphop to dance, reggae to trance, we have the music and the skills to get your nightclub the most talked about party on social media!

Lets Get Down to Business

Is your company hosting a conference? We offer the mics, amplification and visual equipment to make your meeting a productive one.

Is your company throwing a party? We have the necessary music and mic skills to make your corporate event a fun and successful one! We know the perfect songs to play to make everyone in your company, no matter their background or position, want to get up and dance their office stresses away!

House Party!

There are times when we need a dj for small and simple events like house warmings, baptisms, birthday parties, going-away parties and all other special events that get all of your friends and family together.

These are the kind of parties we started out doing and we're happy to go back to these types of parties all the time! We know you may not want to spend the amount of a wedding package but we'll give you and your guests the feeling that you wanted the same effect, for a lot less price.
